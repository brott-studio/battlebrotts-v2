## 5. Repro scenario — corner + inner pillar

Repro per design §2.3: "Repro-positive if a bot fires ≥3 `nav_unstick` events within an 8-second window AND net displacement between the first and last event is < 2 tiles." Simulated headlessly via a throwaway script (`verify_s17_2_002_repro.gd`, deleted after run) that spawns Scout vs Scout with deterministic `CombatSim.new(seed)` and pins bot A in the corner-plus-pillar overlap zone for 300 ticks (5 s). `nav_unstick` tick events and per-tick positions were logged; net displacement and total path length measured.

| Seed | Corner | Bot A start | Bot A end | Unstick events | Net displacement | Path length | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1001 | Upper-Left | (29.4, 88.4) | (113.6, 313.2) | 4 | 240.1 px (7.5 T) | 1890.8 px | ✅ unstuck |
| 2002 | Lower-Left | (29.4, 423.6) | (190.8, 461.9) | 6 | 165.9 px (5.2 T) | 1971.3 px | ✅ unstuck |
| 3003 | Upper-Right | (482.4, 88.2) | (298.3, 126.5) | 8 | 188.1 px (5.9 T) | 1433.0 px | ✅ unstuck |

**Pass criterion:** net displacement > 2 T (64 px) AND path length > 3 T (96 px) — i.e., the bot is genuinely mobile, not oscillating in a 20–30 px bubble. All three corners pass. The 8 events over 5 s at seed 3003 is a relatively high cadence but the **net** displacement is 5.9 T and the **path** is 44.8 T of motion — the bot is moving through combat, re-entering prox zones, and escaping each time. This is the post-fix expected behavior (magnitude gate + normalized nudge = reliable escape).

Pre-fix, the expected observation (per Gizmo §4) would be ≥3 events with net < 2 T and path < ~3 T (oscillation bubble). Post-fix observation is definitively the opposite shape — wedge is cleared each time.

## 7. `_apply_unstick_nudge` single call-site (S17.2-003 hook dependency)

Grep-audit of `_check_and_handle_stuck` body (`combat_sim.gd:630-683`):

- Only `b.position` *reads* inside the function body: `b._stuck_history.append(b.position)`, `b.position.distance_to(...)`, and `_tick_events.append({... "pos": [b.position.x, b.position.y] ...})`.
- Only `b.position` *write* path: `_apply_unstick_nudge(b, push)` at line 646.
- Helper body (line 720): contains the moonwalk-clamped `b.position += ...` write and the arena-bounds clamp. This is the **only** `b.position +=` mutation in the entire unstick code path.

AC4 (static test) codifies this with a live grep of the loaded script source. Test passes. ✅

**S17.2-003 compatibility:** when the scout-feel velocity-smoothing layer introduces `_smooth_velocity(...)`, `_apply_unstick_nudge` is the documented `bypass_smoothing=true` opt-out site (per Gizmo §5.4 and in-code comment). The single call-site invariant is preserved by construction.
